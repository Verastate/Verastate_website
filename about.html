The backstory

Where the idea was born

2markets
South Africa & United States — two of the world's most dynamic real estate landscapes.
1 platform
For agents, agencies, brands, and buyers — all under one roof.

Real estate is, at its core, a people business. But for years, the tools available to professionals in this industry have been fragmented, impersonal, and built for everyone except the agent actually closing deals.

Verastate was founded on a simple observation: agents are doing extraordinary work every day — negotiating, connecting, educating, serving — yet they had no dedicated space to build professional presence, share knowledge, and grow alongside peers.

"Real estate professionals deserve a platform that takes them as seriously as they take their work."

So we built one. Verastate combines a professional networking layer — think of it as LinkedIn built specifically for real estate — with a live property marketplace where listings reach buyers who are already engaged with the industry ecosystem.

The result is a single destination where professionals grow their network, showcase their track record, list their properties, and connect with buyers who are ready to make a move. No more scattered tools. No more shouting into a void. Just a focused, professional community built for real estate, and real results.

Jonnathan Crawford
Founder & CEO, Verastate
Meet the founder

Jonnathan Crawford

Founder & CEO

Jonnathan Crawford is the founder and driving force behind Verastate. With a background spanning technology, entrepreneurship, and a genuine passion for the real estate industry, he built Verastate to bridge a gap he saw firsthand — the lack of a true professional home for real estate agents and agencies.

Jonnathan recognized that the fragmented tools available to real estate professionals were holding the industry back. Agents were using social media platforms not built for them, listing portals with no community, and email chains that went nowhere. He set out to build something different: a platform that puts the professional first.

Beyond the networking layer, Jonnathan's vision for Verastate's property marketplace reflects his belief that buying a home should feel as connected and informed as any major life decision — where buyers engage directly with professionals, and agents get the audience their listings deserve.

Based across South Africa and the United States, Jonnathan is building Verastate with a long-term vision: to become the definitive professional network for real estate in both markets — and eventually, the world.

"I built Verastate because I believe the professionals who make real estate happen deserve a platform that works as hard as they do."

— Jonnathan Crawford, Founder & CEO
What we stand for

Our values

Every decision at Verastate is shaped by a few core beliefs about how professionals deserve to be served.

01
Professionals first

We build for real estate professionals — their needs, workflows, and ambitions drive every product decision we make. No generic SaaS thinking, no afterthoughts.

02
Radical transparency

From market data to platform pricing, we believe professionals perform better when they have access to real information — not curated narratives.

03
Community over competition

The best professionals make the whole industry better. We build features that encourage knowledge sharing, mentorship, and genuine connection — not just self-promotion.

04
Buyers deserve better too

A home purchase is one of the biggest decisions a person makes. We connect buyers directly with the professionals and listings that match what they're looking for — with context, not just listings.

05
Built to last

We're not building for a quick exit. Verastate is a long-term platform investment in an industry that is only growing. We move deliberately, and we build things that hold up.

06
Two markets, one standard

Whether you're in Cape Town or Chicago, the Verastate experience is the same: professional, clean, and built to make you look your best and perform at your highest.
